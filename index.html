Pinjian Chen

This site is still a work in progress (but then again, so am I).

I am Pinjian Chen (陈品健), a first-year Ph.D. student at the National Astronomical Observatories, Chinese Academy of Sciences (NAOC). I completed my undergraduate studies in astrophysics at Yunnan University, where I also greatly benefited from the guidance and support of the Southwest Institute for Astronomy Research (SWIFAR).

You can find my CV here (probably out-of-date, sorry).

I'm an observer interested in a variety of topics in astronomy and astrophysics, including

  • Stellar populations, structures and evolutionary history of our closest large neighbors: M31 & M33
  • Late stage evolution of low- and intermediate-mass stars (post-AGB, PNe)
  • Machine learning’s application in astronomy
